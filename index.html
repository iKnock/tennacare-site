About EnatCare

EnatCare is a mobile health (mHealth) application developed to bridge the gap between modern maternal care and traditional cultural practices in Ethiopia. Our mission is to empower expectant mothers and healthcare providers with accessible, accurate, and culturally-aligned pregnancy tools.

Cultural Relevance

We understand the importance of local context in healthcare. That's why EnatCare uses the Ethiopian Calendar—a system that aligns with how millions of Ethiopians track time—to calculate critical obstetric metrics like Gestational Age (GA) and Expected Delivery Date (EDD).

Our Mission

"To improve maternal health outcomes by combining modern medical tools with culturally-relevant technology that speaks the language—and time—of our communities."
